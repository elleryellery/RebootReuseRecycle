The Lamar High School Computer Science Honor Society is pleased to provide resources for those interested in reducing electronic waste in their environment.

Join us on our journey to deter e-waste on our planet by navigating and sharing this site and adopting e-waste prevention practices.


About

The Lamar CSHS E-Waste Awareness Campaign was designed and developed in 2024 by four high school students in Houston, Texas. The project is intended to increase awareness and action to stop e-waste in our community. For more information about the Lamar CSHS, please visit our "Lamar CSHS" page.


Organize an E-Waste Campaign

We encourage other Computer Science Honor Society chapters to start e-waste initiatives in their own communities, and we have designed some resource to help other campaigns get started! For more information on designing your own campaign, visit our page about reducing e-waste at home.
